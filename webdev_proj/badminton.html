About

Welcome to the Badminton Club at BML Munjal University! We are passionate about badminton and welcome players of all skill levels. Our club is committed to fostering teamwork, improving skills, and having fun on the court. We regularly host training sessions, friendly matches, and exciting tournaments, giving members the chance to showcase their skills and connect with fellow players.
