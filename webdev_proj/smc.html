About

The Social Media Club at BML Munjal University is a hub for creativity and digital innovation, where students collaborate to craft compelling content and amplify the university's online presence. Members gain hands-on experience in social media management, digital marketing, and storytelling, capturing and sharing the vibrant life on campus.

Get Involved

Join the Social Media Club at BML Munjal University to enhance your digital marketing skills, create engaging content, and capture the pulse of campus life. Be part of a dynamic team that showcases the best of BMU to the world!
